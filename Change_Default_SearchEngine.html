Steps to change default search engine from google.co.uk to google.co.in at firefox.exe/firefox browser.
Open firefox.exe
Click view => Toolbar => Customize toolbar
DRAG AND DROP THE SEARCH BAR AT TOP RIGHT CORNER.
Open following url at firefox.exe
https://mycroftproject.com/install.html?id=51876&basename=google_https_co_in&icontype=ico&name=Google+India
01. Click Search button at top right corner.
02. Select Google UK
03. Click Settings.
At left pane click Search
Drill down google and select Google UK
