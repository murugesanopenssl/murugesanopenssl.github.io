STEPS TO PAY PROPERTY TAX BENGALURU (PANCHAYATH/RURAL AREA)
IF YOUR OWN PROPERTY/HOUSE BELONGS TO PANCHAYATH AREA IN BENGALURU, YOU CAN PAY PROPERTY TAX USING BELOW PORTAL:
https://bsk.karnataka.gov.in
FIRST REGISTER IN THE ABOVE PORTAL AND PROCEED WITH TAX PAYMENT.
STEPS TO PAY PROPERTY TAX BENGALURU (NON-PANCHAYATH/BBMP AREA)
IF YOUR OWN PROPERTY/HOUSE DOES NOT BELONG TO PANCHAYATH AREA, USE FOLLOWING PORTAL:
https://bbmptax.karnataka.gov.in
COMPLETE THE PAYMENT PROCESS IN THE ABOVE WEBSITE.
STEPS TO PAY PROPERTY TAX DHARMAPURI TOWN (TAMIL NADU)
FOR PROPERTIES LOCATED IN DHARMAPURI TOWN OR OTHER MUNICIPALITIES IN TAMIL NADU, USE THE TN URBAN PAY PORTAL:
https://tnurbanpay.tn.gov.in
ENTER YOUR ASSESSMENT NUMBER OR MOBILE NUMBER TO SEARCH AND PAY.
STEPS TO PAY PROPERTY TAX TELANGANA (CDMA/GHMC)
FOR TELANGANA STATE MUNICIPALITIES AND GHMC AREAS, USE THE OFFICIAL PORTAL BELOW:
https://cdma.telangana.gov.in
SELECT YOUR DISTRICT AND ULB TO PROCEED WITH THE PAYMENT.
STEPS TO PAY PROPERTY TAX ANDHRA PRADESH (CDMA AP)
FOR PROPERTIES IN ANDHRA PRADESH MUNICIPALITIES, USE THE COMMISSIONER AND DIRECTOR OF MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION PORTAL:
https://cdma.ap.gov.in
USE THE 'ONLINE PAYMENTS' SECTION TO PAY YOUR PROPERTY TAX.
