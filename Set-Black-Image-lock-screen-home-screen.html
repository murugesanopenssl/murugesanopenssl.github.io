Samsung M35 Mobile Android 16 Black Wallpaper Permanent Fix.
Samsung M07 Mobile Android 16 Black Wallpaper Permanent Fix.
Step 1 Create Black Image Open Camera
Place the mobile on a table or black cloth
Take a photo
Rename the photo as Background.jpg
Close Camera
Step 2 Apply Wallpaper Open Settings
Scroll down and tap Wallpaper and style
Two images will be displayed
Select the left image
Tap Gallery
Select the image from Today
Tap Done.
Step 3 Disable Wallpaper Service Below the two images tap Change wallpapers
Scroll down
Tap Wallpaper services
Select None
Tap Apply.
Result Lock screen and home screen will always display the same black image
