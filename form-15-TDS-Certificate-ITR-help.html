Answer Explanation Status
Form 16A showing zero TDS means bank has not deducted tax and document is only informational X
Form 16A is not required to be uploaded or submitted while filing Income Tax Return(ITR) X
Form 15G is used only to avoid TDS deduction and is not required in Income Tax Return(ITR) submission X
FD interest for which Form 15G was submitted is **not taxable and TDS is zero**, but can be recorded for completeness if desired ✔
None of the documents like SBI PDFs or Form15G files are required to upload during Income Tax Return(ITR) ✔
MYPAN quarterly PDFs, latest Form15G, and all Form15G files are not required for Income Tax Return(ITR) filing X
Both old and new SBI accounts must be considered as income is linked to same PAN ✔
PERFORM FOLLOWING ACTION AFTER Sun 31-May-2026 IST
Total interest can be downloaded from SBI internet banking using interest certificate option for FY 2025-26, or verified from bank statement or AIS		 ✔
