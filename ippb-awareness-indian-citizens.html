IPPB Account Type Awareness For Indian Citizens

This page is created to inform people about issues faced when an IPPB Premium Account is opened instead of a Normal Savings Account.

Case Summary

An IPPB account was opened approximately on or before Tue 19-Aug-2025 IST.

The account holder is an uneducated senior citizen and was not informed that a Premium Account was opened instead of a Normal Savings Account.

The Premium Account information was discovered only during Jan-2026 IST.

Amount Deducted

Date Amount Reason Given By IPPB
16-Nov-2025 116.82 Service Charge
15-Dec-2025 92.64 Service Charge

Interaction At IPPB Post Office

When questioned at IPPB Post Office Dharmapuri, staff stated that service charges cannot be prevented and are also deducted for IPPB employees.

This interaction was recorded on the IPPB office webcam.

Action Taken

A written request was submitted to IPPB to change the account from Premium to Normal Savings Account.

This change was requested to prevent further unwanted deductions.

Public Advisory

Indian citizens are advised to clearly confirm that a Normal Savings Account is opened at IPPB.

Please do not assume the account type, especially for elderly or uneducated customers.

Always request written confirmation of account category and applicable charges.

This awareness can help avoid unnecessary service charge deductions.
