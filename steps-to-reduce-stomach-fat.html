Steps To Reduce Stomach Fat
Daily Exercises
Follow these exercises regularly to reduce stomach fat and improve fitness.
  • Start walking for 5 minutes on first date. Increase the walking time daily by 5 minutes extra.No need to increase the time once reaching 45 minutes.
  • Stand, raise your left and right knees high for 10 times during first day.Daily increase by 1 more time.No need to increase the count after reaching 35 times.
  • Stand, try to sit down without touching the floor. Initially do one time on first day.Daily increase by 1 more time.No need to increase the count after reaching 35 times.
  • Hold plank position for 20 to 30 seconds.
  • Do bicycle crunches slowly.
  • Use stairs for extra calorie burning.
Flexibility Exercises
  • Touch toes without bending legs.
  • Sit without touching buttocks to floor for 30 seconds.
  • Expand hands from chest and return slowly.
  • Rotate left and right legs carefully.
Food Tips
  • Reduce sugar and fried foods.
  • Drink enough water daily.
  • Eat more vegetables and protein.
  • Avoid eating late at night.
Stay consistent and active every day.
