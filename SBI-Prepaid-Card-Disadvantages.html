Using SBI internet banking, we can apply for the SBI prepaid card online.
If we apply for the SBI prepaid card, we need to have a balance on the prepaid card, which is deducted from the owner's SBI account.
SBI Prepaid Card Disadvantages
No SBI Rewards Prepaid cards do not offer the reward points that come with SBI Debit Cards.
AMB Requirement Some prepaid cards may have an Average Monthly Balance (AMB) requirement, which could be a hassle.
Reduced Balance in Main Account The prepaid card is loaded from your main account, which reduces your available balance in the account.
Potential Service Charges There might be service charges in the future, such as fees for reloading or card maintenance.
Extra Data for Security You may need to install and manage mobile apps, taking up space or data, to track and secure the prepaid card.
Note: I have never applied for the SBI Prepaid Card and will not apply for it in the future due to the disadvantages listed above.
