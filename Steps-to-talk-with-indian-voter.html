Steps to Talk with Indian Voter Help from any district inside Tamilnadu India

Follow these steps to reach a human agent for voter assistance:

  1. Dial the toll-free number: 1800-425-21950
  2. Press 1 for Tamil language.
  3. Press 6 to talk with a human agent.
  4. Wait patiently as it may ring multiple times before connecting.
  5. If the call disconnects, try again after a few minutes or use the online portal.

The best time to call is between 9:30 AM and 11:30 AM IST or 3:00 PM and 5:00 PM IST to avoid busy lines.
