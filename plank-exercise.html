Wall Plank Exercise (Beginner Startup Guide)
How to Do the Exercise:

    The Setup: Stand facing a wall. Keep your feet spaced apart so they align perfectly with your shoulders and hands.

    The Distance: Step back approximately one arm's length away from the wall so that you can just touch it with your hands.

    The Form: Place your hands on the wall. Keep your fingers slightly bent to grip and protect your wrists, but keep your arms straight and firm throughout the exercise.

    The Movement: Hold your body rigid and straight like a plank. Slowly lean your upper body toward the wall (keeping your heels slightly lifted or flat as comfortable) and then push back to the straight starting position.

Your Daily Repetition Schedule

You will build your strength gradually by adding 2 repetitions every day. Once you reach 50, you will stay at that number to maintain your fitness.

    Day 1: 10 times

    Day 2: 12 times

    Day 3: 14 times

    Day 4: 16 times

    (Keep adding 2 each day)

    Target Day: 50 times

    ⚠️ Important: Once you reach 50 times, do not increase the count any further.

This is a fantastic startup routine. It allows your body to adapt to the plank position without overloading your joints!
