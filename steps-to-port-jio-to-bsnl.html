Mobile Number Porting to BSNL

01) Never send the PORT message from another person’s mobile number.
02) Always send the SMS from your own mobile number only.
03) Send the SMS to 1900 from your number after reaching the BSNL or Airtel office:
        a) At BSNL office, the UPC is valid for 3 days only (not 5 days).
        b) At Airtel office, the UPC is valid for 4 days only (not 5 days).

Before Reaching BSNL Office

If you are porting to BSNL,first travel to BSNL office.

Before reaching BSNL office send SMS:

PORT <YOUR MOBILE NUMBER WITHOUT +91> to 1900

You will receive UPC(Unique Porting Code) on the same day.

Use that UPC on the same day at BSNL office.

BSNL Office:UPC must be submitted within 3 days only and not 5 days.

Airtel Office:UPC must be submitted within 4 days only and not 5 days.
