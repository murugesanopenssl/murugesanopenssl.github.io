LPG Subsidy Fix: Sister's Connection

Current Status:
  • Mother's House: Receiving (37.79 ರೂ/ரூ/₹ INR) due to PAHAL Scheme registration by my mother.
  • Sister's House(after her marriage using different ration card): NOT Receiving

Because both connections are under your father's Aadhaar, the system only pays out to one primary account. Your sister needs her own "Subsidy Identity."

Step 1: Transfer Name to Sister

The connection must be moved from your Father's name to your Sister's name. As long as it is in your father's name, the computer(from government) sees it as a "second connection" for him and may refuse the subsidy.

Documents: Sister’s Aadhaar, her separate Ration Card (proof of her new home), and a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from your father.

Step 2: Biometric e-KYC

Your sister must visit the gas agency in person to do a Face Scan or Fingerprint. This updates the 2026 database to show she is the actual user at the second address, disconnecting the link to your father's ID.

Step 3: Bank Account Link (IPPB)

Request the agency to link your sister's IPPB Account as the primary "DBT" (Direct Benefit Transfer) account. This ensures the 37.79 ರೂ/ரூ/₹ INR goes to her bank and not your father’s or mother’s account.

Check Manually: Do not rely on BSNL SMS notifications. Visit the Post Office or use the IPPB app to check for the subsidy credit 3-5 days after your next cylinder delivery.
