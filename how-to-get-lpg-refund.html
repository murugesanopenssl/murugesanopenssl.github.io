LPG Subsidy Restoration Plan for 1st Mum

Since both gas connections are in your father's name, the system has blocked the 1st house subsidy as a duplicate. Follow these steps to fix it permanently.

Step 1: Transfer Connection Name

Visit the Gas Agency to transfer the connection from your Father's name to your 1st Mum's name. Because the system allows only one subsidized connection per Aadhaar, she must become the official owner. Bring the 1st Mum's separate Ration Card, her Aadhaar, and your Father's Aadhaar with a signed consent letter for the transfer.

Step 2: Biometric e-KYC Update

While at the Gas Agency, the 1st Mum must perform a Biometric Authentication (Thumb print or Face scan). This proves to the 2026 automated system that she is a separate, living individual at a distinct address. This will clear the "Duplicate Flag" currently caused by your father's Voter ID being at the 2nd house.

Step 3: Bank Seeding Verification

Request the agent to link the 1st Mum's IPPB Account specifically to her new Consumer ID. Once the name transfer is complete, the "subsidy pipe" will point to her account instead of your father's. After booking the next cylinder, the refund of ₹39.79 will automatically deposit into her IPPB account.

Note: Do not rely on BSNL SMS alerts for now. Visit the Post Office to check the balance manually until the transfer is finished.
