--- The Original GeoCities Service ---
The original GeoCities service was launched in 1994 and was later acquired by Yahoo! (owned by Apollo Global Management) in
1999. Key points about the original service:
* --- Founders: --- David Bohnett and John Rezner
* --- Original Name: --- Beverly Hills Internet, later changed to GeoCities
* --- Acquisition: --- Acquired by Yahoo! in 1999
* --- Service: --- A web hosting service that allowed users to create and publish websites
* --- Unique Feature: --- Users could choose a "city" (e.g. Silicon Valley or Hollywood) to categorize their websites
* --- Inactive: --- The original GeoCities service was shut down by Yahoo! on October 26, 2009
