The concept of numbered buffers :
    This can be used to restore up to nine complete line deletions.
    Buffers are numbered from 1 to 9.
    The most recent deletion will be in buffer numbered 1, the next most in buffer numbered 2 and so on.
    Here only entire lines can be restored and not Parts of lines.
    To restore the most recently deleted group of lines , move the cursor to the desired location and use
        "1p
    and so on.
Example:
echo -n > delete.txt
for VAL in {1..10}
do
   echo $VAL >> delete.txt
done
/usr/bin/vim delete.txt
