LED Bulb Repair Report

I repaired the LED bulb manually by opening it using my hand and inspecting the internal LED board.

The LED bulb had 32 LEDs in total.

LED arrangement:

First(outermost) round having: 21st, 22nd, 23rd, 24th, 25th, 26th, 27th, 28th, 29th, 31st and 32nd LED bulbs

Second(middle) round: 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th, 5th, 6th, 7th, 8th, 9th, 10th, 11th, 12th, 13th, 14th and 15th LED bulbs

Third(last/internal) round: 16th, 17th, 18th, 19th and 20th LED bulbs

I found that LEDs 9th, 10th and 11th had yellow/black scratches.

I scratched them carefully using a SCREWDRIVER OR SAFETY PIN.

 LED BULB AFTER SCRATCHING?

Then I soldered LED 9, 10, and 11 using a soldering machine.

Before repair, the 2nd and 3rd rounds used to switch off and switch on repeatedly when the LED bulb was turned on.

After soldering and reassembling the LED bulb by hand, the issue was resolved successfully.
