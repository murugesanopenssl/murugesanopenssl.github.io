Always Keep Some Money In Purse/Mobile Cover

The purpose of this page is to remind everyone on Earth to always keep some physical money in Purse/Mobile Cover and not fully depend on GPay, PhonePe, or any mobile payment application.

A Real Life Exception

My mother was traveling from the school to the bus stand and then to my house.

On Sun 01-Feb-2026 (IST), she stepped down from the bus and needed to pay the auto driver (Kannada, Karnataka, India).

She was unable to pay using GPay.

The reason was that State Bank of India services were scheduled to be down on Sun 01-Feb-2026 from 06:45 AM to 07:30 AM IST.

Even I was unable to transfer the money to that auto driver.

My mother knew Kannada and explained the situation.

The best auto driver(kannada) agreed to receive the money later after 01-Feb-2026 at 07:35 AM.

Lesson

Technology is helpful but not guaranteed at all times.
Always having some cash in Purse/Mobile Cover can help during unexpected situations including emergencies..
