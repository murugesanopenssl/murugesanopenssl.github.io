Always Keep Some Money In Purse/Mobile Cover

This page reminds everyone on Earth to always keep some physical money in Purse/Mobile Cover and not fully depend on GPay, PhonePe, or any mobile payment application.

A Real Life Exception

My mother traveled from the school to the bus stand using auto, then from the bus stand to my house.

On Sun 01-Feb-2026 (IST), she stepped down from the auto and needed to pay the auto driver (Kannada, Karnataka, India).

She was unable to pay using GPay.

The reason: State Bank of India services were scheduled to be down on Sun 01-Feb-2026 from 06:45 AM to 07:30 AM IST.

Even I was unable to transfer the money to that auto driver.

My mother knew Kannada and explained the situation.

The best auto driver (Kannada) agreed to receive the money later after 01-Feb-2026 at 07:35 AM.
My mother started travelling using the bus to my house from 01-Feb-2026 06:54 AM IST.
Using my mobile payment, I transferred 61.00 INR to that auto driver on Sun 01-Feb-2026 07:39 AM IST
After this payment, I shared my payment details to the auto driver via mobile call.
My mother reached my home at 01-Feb-2026 08:33 AM.

Lesson

Technology is helpful but not guaranteed at all times.
Always having some cash in Purse/Mobile Cover can help during unexpected situations including emergencies..
