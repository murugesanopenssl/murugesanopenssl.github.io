How to Order Aadhaar PVC Card Online Without Broker

You can order your Aadhaar PVC card yourself from UIDAI website. No broker is required.

Note: Always use the official UIDAI website.

Step 1: Open UIDAI Website

Open:
https://uidai.gov.in

Step 2: Select "Order Aadhaar PVC Card"

Open Aadhaar Services and click:
Order Aadhaar PVC Card

Step 3: Login

Enter:
  • Aadhaar Number
  • Captcha
Click: Login with OTP

Step 4: Enter OTP

Enter the OTP received on your Aadhaar registered mobile number.

Click Login.

Step 5: Order PVC Card

After login, click:
Order Aadhaar PVC Card
Then click Next.

Step 6: Confirm and Pay

Accept the declaration and continue.


UIDAI will show the current PVC card charges.
Complete online payment.

I have paid using gpay at:

Click download acknowledgement.

Step 7: Delivery

The PVC Aadhaar card will be delivered by post to your Aadhaar registered address.

Why avoid brokers?

  • You can order directly from UIDAI.
  • No extra service charge is needed.
  • Your Aadhaar details remain with UIDAI.
