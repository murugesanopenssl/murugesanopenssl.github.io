Steps to Pay Property Tax Bengaluru (Panchayath/Rural Area)
If your own property/house belongs to Panchayath area in Bengaluru, you can pay property tax using below portal:
https://bsk.karnataka.gov.in
First register in the above portal and proceed with tax payment.
Steps to Pay Property Tax Bengaluru (Non-Panchayath/BBMP Area)
If your own property/house does not belong to Panchayath area, use following portal:
https://bbmptax.karnataka.gov.in
Complete the payment process in the above website.
My Payment Experience at pancyahath during 2025
01) During 2025 I had not paid my property tax for my own house from 2021 to 2025.
Initially, a Bengaluru KHB Housing Board government officer advised me to register the property in my name instead of the seller’s name for the BESCOM current bill.
I visited the Attibele BESCOM office, where they directed me to another BESCOM office located about 1.5 KM away.
On Mon 09-Jun-2025 IST, I paid 400.00 ರೂ/ரூ/₹ INR as an unofficial payment to a helper (BESCOM bill-related person) at that office to assist with the process.
After that, I went back to the Bengaluru KHB Housing Board office and received a receipt for my property tax payment.
Since I had not paid tax for 5 years, the total amount mentioned in the receipt was 43,490.00 ರೂ/ரூ/₹ INR which I was supposed to pay at Union Bank, Chandapura.
02) During 2026 Later, on Sun 05-Apr-2026, I registered my mobile number on:
https://bbmptax.karnataka.gov.in
I belong to a Panchayath area.
I paid my property tax online instead of going through the earlier process.
The amount I paid was 2,981.00 ರೂ/ரூ/₹ INR.
I used mobile payment (GPay) for the transaction.
ATM card payments had a service charge of 157.00 ರೂ/ரூ/₹ INR, so I avoided using an ATM card and chose GPay to save that cost.
