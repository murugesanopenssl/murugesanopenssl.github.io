New Employee Savings Guide

This guide explains how new employees at private companies can manage their salary and savings effectively.

1. Open a Salary Account

  • Open a salary account at SBI (State Bank of India).
  • Reason: SBI is a government bank and provides reliable Internet banking, YONO Lite and YONO SBI mobile apps.
  • We will manage money using banking apps, not by visiting the bank physically.

2. Salary Management

  • Assume your first month salary is ₹21,000.00 .
  • Inform others that you are receiving ₹12,000.00 .
  • Deposit ₹5,000 in Post Office MIS using Internet banking.

3. Monthly Deposits

  • Deposit money from SBI to Post Office on the last day of the month.
  • Deposit the remaining balance before receiving the next month's salary.

4. Opening MIS

  • Whenever your saved amount reaches ₹5,000, open a MIS (Monthly Income Scheme) account at the Post Office.
  • Open MIS sequentially on different days (1st, 2nd, 3rd … 31st) of different months and years.
  • Example: 01-Jan-2026, 02-Mar-2026, 03-Jun-2026, …

5. MIS Interest

  • Since each month has 4–5 Sundays, MIS interest (₹31) will be credited on Saturdays instead of Sundays.
  • Save MIS interest in Post Office itself.
  • Once the saved amount reaches ₹5,000 again, open a new MIS using Internet banking without physically visiting the post office, saving fuel and reducing pollution.
