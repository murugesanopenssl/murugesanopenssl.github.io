Why Should You Open a Fixed Deposit?

We can open any number of fixed deposits (FD/ETDR) at SBI bank using online or inside SBI on the same day.
However, the closing date of each fixed deposit should not be the same date.

Case 1: If I open two fixed deposits with the same closing date, there will be no service charge.
Reason: I will receive the principal and interest twice for those fixed deposits, resulting in 4 transactions, which is under the limit of 5.

 Case 2: If I open three fixed deposits with the same closing date, I will receive three times the principal and three times of related ETDR interest.
However, since this results in 6 transactions ( greater than 5 ), SBI will automatically apply a service charge.
