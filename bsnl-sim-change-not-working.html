BSNL SIM Change Voice Cutting Problem Fixed
I transferred my BSNL SIM from Tamil Nadu to Karnataka on Thu 04-Sep-2025 IST.
After switching my SIM card, whenever I make a call using my mobile,
there are moments during the conversation—lasting a few seconds—when my voice does not reach the person on the other end.
They would tell me that they cannot hear what I am saying clearly.
I finally found the main reason on Fri 08-May-2026 IST.
Solution
BSNL Message Centre Lookup
Select your state to see the BSNL Message Centre number:
BSNL Message Centre Number: +919448099987
Open Messages.
Go to Settings.
Click More Settings.
Open Message Centre.
The old message centre number was: +91 9442099997
I changed the number to: +91 9448099997
After changing the message centre number, my BSNL SIM voice call problem was fixed successfully.
My BSNL SIM calls started working properly from Fri 08-May-2026 IST after updating the message centre number.
