Purpose:
01. Do not waste money purchasing additional email storage unless it is really required.
02. Regularly clean unnecessary emails to keep your mailbox within the free storage limit whenever possible.
03. Before deleting important emails, print them as PDF or save their attachments to localhost as a backup.
04. After deleting emails, empty the Trash and Spam folders. Review the Archive folder and remove emails that are no longer required.
My monthly email cleanup workflow (since 2003 to till now): 01. I use Mozilla Thunderbird/Microsoft OUTLOOK on my personal laptop and my mobile (localhost / 127.0.0.1).
02. I configure all my email accounts and my family members' email accounts in Thunderbird.
03. On the 1st day of every month, I search emails by Subject and From address, delete emails that are no longer required, and save important emails as PDF when necessary.
04. After the cleanup, I review and clean the Trash, Spam, and Archive folders for all configured email accounts.
