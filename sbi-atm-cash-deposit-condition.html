If any person performs cash deposit using SBI ATM before 10:00 AM or after 04:00 PM on any day (Monday to Sunday), SBI computer system may automatically perform service charge deduction from that person's SBI account.

PERFORM CASH DEPOSIT AFTER 10:00 AM AND BEFORE 04:00 PM USING SBI ATM

Performing cash deposit using SBI ATM after 10:00 AM and before 04:00 PM is recommended.

REASON

SBI ATM cash deposit transactions are processed according to the bank's internal computer system timing. Depositing within the suggested time period can help avoid unexpected service charges.
